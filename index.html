CAFFEINA COFFEE ROASTING COMPANY IN BOISE, IDAHO

We are small batch Boise, Idaho coffee roaster and coffee shop focused on blending new roasting and brewing methods with traditional roasting and brewing methods, blending brewing styles and developing authentic profiles; sourcing all free market coffees, small farm, certified organics and fair trade. We prioritize educating our team in all brewing methods to ensure our coffees are presented in the finest form.

Our passion is coffee and our obsession is life, nature and health. We’re committed to brewing, all brewing. Blending the craft beer scene with the craft coffee world creating a northwest inspired craft brew bar located in Boise, Idaho. We’re committed to innovation, unfolding the controlled and discovering new limits and profiles within the current coffee standards. We’re inspired by living things, nature reminds us to stay present, kombucha reminds us to take care of your gut.
